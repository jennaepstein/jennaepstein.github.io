Predicting Wildfire Risk and Preparing Communities in Southern California

For this project, Jeff Stern and I developed a wildfire risk prediction model and interactive application for evacuation kit distribution in Southern California. Incorporating environmental and spatial risk factors to build accuracy and generalizability, our linear model outputs probabilities of wildfire occurrence to represent risk. Our interactive application combines the census tract-level wildfire risk with CDC's Social Vulnerability Index scores in a final algorithm; based on user inputs, the application provides a prioritized list of tracts to target for emergency supply kit distribution.
