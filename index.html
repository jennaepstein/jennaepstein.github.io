Forecasting Flood Inundation in Calgary and Denver

View Project Markdown | View Summary

My team developed a machine learning algorithm to predict which areas of a city are at the highest risk of flood inundation. This model was developed using 2013 flood extent data from Calgary and iteratively tested to achieve optimal accuracy, and applied to Denver to assess its generalizability.
